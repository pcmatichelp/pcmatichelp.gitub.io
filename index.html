Setting Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your PC Matic account login with 2FA. Even if someone gets your password, they can't access your account without the second factor.

Step 1
Go to Account Settings → Security
Step 2
Find "Two-Factor Authentication" and click "Enable"
Step 3
Scan the QR code with authenticator app
Step 4
Enter the 6-digit verification code from the app
Step 5
Click "Enable" to activate 2FA
Step 6
Save recovery codes in a secure location
⚠️ Important: Store your recovery codes in a password manager or physical safe. Without them, you could lose access to your account if you lose your authenticator app.

Common PC Matic Account Login Issues & Fixes

Here are the most common login problems and their solutions:

❌ Invalid Credentials
Check for typos in email/password. Passwords are case-sensitive. Use "Show Password" to verify.
❌ 2FA Code Not Working
Sync your device clock. Codes expire every 30 seconds. Try the next code or use a recovery code.
❌ Account Locked
Wait 15-30 minutes after multiple failed attempts. Use password reset or contact support.
❌ Browser/App Not Loading
Clear cache and cookies. Update browser/app. Try different browser or incognito mode.
❌ "Account Not Found"
Verify you're using the correct email address. You may have registered with a different email.
❌ Session Timeout
PC Matic automatically logs you out after inactivity. Adjust timeout settings in preferences.
